    • COGNIZANCE 2017

    Born with the motto of promoting technology, creative thinking and innovation, COGNIZANCE is the most celebrated Tech-Symposium of ComputerScience Department KEC. It creates an unmatched amalgam of technology, creativity and festivity. It brings you immense opportunities to showcase your talent and skills. With enormous number of enthusiasts facing off under one roof, it is a spark created by the meet of exuberance and technology.

    Characterised by entertainment, it is sure to rejuvenate the insatiable souls of technophiles. Once you are here, you are no more a student, but a learner for whom there are lots kept in store to explore. Learn and take away with you a truly enriching experience.

    Join us in this celebration of innovation and technology and leave your mark by solving the mind blogging challenges. We wish you a very Good Luck !
